Transcribe interviews with ease

Transcribing is a tedious and long process, and getting someone else to do it for you, is expensive and takes a long time.

With Mellonn Speak You can upload your recording and let the AI do the hard work for you, It's as easy as that!

Creating the transcription

We won't tell you how to do your interview, that's why we support 34 different languages/accents, and 9 different file types.

Then once You've recorded your interview, you can upload it in Mellonn Speak, then the AI will do it's magic.

Editing the transcription

As we all know, AIs isn't perfect, and we won't lie this one isn't either.

That's why we didn't stop at getting an AI to transcribe an interview and then exporting it. We wanted to make it just as easy to edit the transcription afterwards.

You'll let the AI do the tedious parts of the job, and then You can change what needs changing, using the easy to use tools in Speak's arsenal.

Listen while you edit

While editing a transcription, You can always listen to the recording. Speak will automatically select the part where you're currently editing.

Exporting the transcription

Once you're done editing the transcription, you can always export it and use it where ever you'll need it!

Export as Word-document

You can always export the transcription as a Word-document (DOCX). DOCX-files are primarily used for Word-documents, but can also be use in a wide variety of other text editors.
We're constantly working on adding other file types, if you have suggestions feel free to let us know!

Your data is save with us

We only store the data you give us, and we can't identify any recordings uploaded to Mellonn Speak.

Six months after a recording have been uploaded, they will be removed along with all the associated data. And if you want, You can always remove a recording or your account completely, this action won't take more than a minute, and we won't try and stop you!
